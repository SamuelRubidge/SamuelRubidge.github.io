R.E.A.A. Version 1

The predecessor to the Real Estate Acquisition Advisor was a robust Excel-based property tracking and financial analysis tool. This comprehensive solution allowed users to meticulously monitor and evaluate properties of interest by conducting in-depth calculations, including cash flow simulations, Home Price Index (HPI) scenarios, and holding scenarios. With its data-driven approach, this Excel tool provided valuable insights for real estate investors, empowering them to make informed decisions in a dynamic property market environment. This precursor project laid the groundwork for the subsequent development of the Real Estate Acquisition Advisor.
